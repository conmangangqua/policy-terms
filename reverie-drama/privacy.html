Privacy Policy

App: Reverie Drama Effective: 09/07/2026 Last Updated: 09/07/2026

Reverie Drama ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

The App is an entertainment app that streams short-form drama series. You do not need to create an account to use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you for your name, email, phone number, or address. We do not require an account. We (and our third-party partners) automatically collect the following when you use the App:

  • Device and other identifiers — advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID), device/app instance identifiers, IP address.
  • Approximate location — a coarse, city/region-level location derived from your IP address by advertising partners (no GPS/precise location is collected).
  • App activity — how you interact with the App (screens viewed, episodes watched, features used) and in-app search queries you enter.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — technical data about crashes, errors, and performance to keep the App stable.
  • Purchase information — the status of subscriptions/purchases made through Google Play (we do not receive your full payment card details; those are handled by Google Play).

We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, SMS, or health data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and stream content;
  • Remember your progress ("continue watching") and personalize recommendations;
  • Show advertising, including measuring and (where permitted) personalizing ads;
  • Analyze usage and improve features and performance;
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security, and technical issues;
  • Process subscriptions and comply with legal obligations.

Ad personalization & consent

Where required (e.g. in the EEA/UK), we present a consent choice through Google's User Messaging Platform before serving personalized ads. You can change your choice at any time in the App's privacy/ad settings.

Control your advertising ID

You can reset or delete your advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking / Apple Advertising).

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We share data:

  • With the third-party services listed above, to provide advertising, analytics, attribution, and payments;
  • For legal reasons — to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect rights, safety, and security;
  • In a business transfer — as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to this Policy.

Some sharing for advertising may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under certain U.S. state laws (see Section 7).

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

Where GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we rely on: consent (for personalized advertising and, where required, analytics); legitimate interests (app functionality, security, non-personalized measurement, product improvement); and contract/legal obligation (processing purchases, meeting legal duties). You may withdraw consent at any time.

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Analytics and advertising identifiers are retained according to our partners' retention periods; crash/diagnostic data is retained for a limited period to resolve issues.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, to object to or restrict processing, and to withdraw consent.

  • EEA/UK (GDPR): the rights above, plus the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): the right to know, delete, correct, and to opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information and targeted advertising. We do not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.
  • Other regions (e.g. Brazil LGPD, Canada PIPEDA): equivalent rights under local law.

To exercise any right, or to request deletion of data associated with your device/advertising ID, contact us at contact@tranquilmind.co. Because the App has no account, please include your advertising ID or device details so we can locate the data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 16 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age required by local law (e.g. 13 under COPPA, or higher in the EEA). If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. Where required, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Data Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
