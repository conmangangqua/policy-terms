Privacy Policy

App: Dramio Last Updated: July 23, 2026

Dramio ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Dramio is a streaming app for vertical short dramas — bite-size episodes across romance, mafia, billionaire, and werewolf stories. You do not need to create an account to use it; you can start watching immediately.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to provide personal details such as your name or email to use the App. We automatically collect the following information, which leaves your device only through the third-party services listed in Section 3:

  • Device and advertising identifiers — including your device's advertising ID and similar identifiers used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.
  • Approximate location — a coarse, city-level location derived from your IP address by advertising and analytics services. We do not collect precise/GPS location.
  • App activity — how you interact with the App (episodes viewed, screens opened, features used, watch progress). Your searches are processed on your device against the content feed and are not sent to us as a search history.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — technical information about crashes, errors, and performance to help us fix problems.
  • Purchase status — whether you hold an active subscription or entitlement, so we can unlock premium content. Payment card details are handled by Google Play, not by us.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and stream content to you.
  • Remember your watch progress, favorites, language, and preferences.
  • Show and measure advertising, including rewarded ads that unlock content.
  • Understand usage through analytics and improve the App.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Process and validate subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We share information only with the third-party services listed in Section 3 so they can perform their functions, for legal reasons (to comply with law or protect rights and safety), and in connection with a business transfer (e.g. a merger or acquisition).

U.S. state privacy note. Because the App serves personalized advertising, some U.S. state laws (e.g. California's CCPA/CPRA) may treat the sharing of advertising identifiers with ad partners for targeted advertising as a "sale" or "sharing." You can opt out of personalized ads using the ad-consent controls and the device-level advertising-ID controls described in Section 3.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

  • Consent — personalized ads and, where required, analytics.
  • Legitimate interests — core functionality, security and fraud prevention, and improving the App.
  • Contract / legal obligation — processing subscriptions and purchases, and meeting legal requirements.

6. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Advertising and analytics identifiers are retained according to each partner's retention periods; crash and diagnostic data are kept for a limited period to diagnose issues.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, object to, or restrict processing of your personal information, and to withdraw consent.

  • EEA/UK (GDPR): access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, restriction, withdrawal of consent, and the right to complain to a data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale/sharing of personal information and targeted advertising, with no discrimination for exercising your rights.
  • Other regions (e.g. LGPD, PIPEDA): equivalent rights under your local law.

Because the App has no accounts, when you contact us to exercise a right, please include your advertising ID and device details so we can identify the relevant data. Contact us at the email in Section 13.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 16 and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. Where required, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Data Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and other reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Third-Party Content

The App streams video content and may contain links or references to third-party services. We are not responsible for the content, accuracy, or privacy practices of third parties. Please review their policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
