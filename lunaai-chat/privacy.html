Privacy Policy

App: LunaAI Chat Effective: July 12, 2026 Last Updated: July 12, 2026

LunaAI Chat ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

LunaAI Chat is an AI companion and assistant app that lets you chat with AI characters by text or voice. You do not need to create an account to use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to run the App, show ads, and improve the product. We do not collect data we do not use.

Information you provide

  • Chat content. The messages you type or dictate to the AI are sent to our AI provider (DeepSeek) so the App can generate a reply (see Section 3). Recent messages in a conversation may be sent together to give the AI context.
  • Profile & preferences. Your display name, age, gender, chosen avatar, and the interest topics you select are stored on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers; some of these details may be included in the prompt sent to the AI provider to personalize replies.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and advertising identifiers (including the Android Advertising ID) and general device information.
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address by our advertising and analytics partners (we do not collect GPS/precise location).
  • App activity — how you interact with features (e.g. screens viewed, chats started), used for analytics and advertising.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — technical data to detect and fix problems.
  • Purchase status — whether you hold an active subscription (processed through Google Play Billing; we never receive your card or bank details).
Microphone / voice. If you use voice-to-text, the App uses your device's built-in speech-recognition service to convert speech to text. We do not record, store, or upload raw audio; only the resulting text is used as your chat message.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the App and generate AI replies.
  • Remember your preferences and personalize the experience.
  • Show advertising and measure its performance.
  • Analytics and product improvement.
  • Fraud prevention, security, and abuse detection.
  • Process and validate purchases and subscriptions.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We share data with the third-party services listed in Section 3, for the purposes described there; to comply with law or valid legal requests; and in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.

U.S. state privacy note. Because we allow partners to serve personalized ads, some U.S. state laws (e.g. California's CCPA/CPRA) may classify this as "sharing" or "sale" for cross-context behavioral advertising. You can opt out by declining ad personalization in the App's settings and by using the device advertising-ID controls above.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

  • Consent — personalized advertising and, where required, analytics.
  • Legitimate interests — core functionality, security, fraud prevention, and product improvement.
  • Performance of a contract / legal obligation — processing purchases and meeting legal requirements.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Advertising and analytics identifiers are retained according to each partner's retention periods; crash and diagnostic data are kept for a limited period. Chat content is processed to generate replies and may be cached on your device; server-side retention by the AI provider is governed by its own policy.

7. Your Rights

GDPR (EEA/UK): access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, restriction, and withdrawal of consent, plus the right to complain to your data protection authority.

CCPA/CPRA (California): the right to know, delete, and correct; to opt out of the "sale"/"sharing" of personal information and targeted advertising; and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

Other regions: residents of Brazil (LGPD), Canada (PIPEDA), and other jurisdictions have comparable rights.

To exercise any right, email us at contact@tranquilmind.co. Because the App has no account, please include your Android Advertising ID and relevant device details so we can locate and act on your data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 16 years of age and older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age of digital consent. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Data Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. AI-Generated Content

Replies in the App are generated by artificial intelligence and may be inaccurate or unexpected. They are provided for general conversation and entertainment and are not professional medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
