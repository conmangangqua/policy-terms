Privacy Policy

App: DreamRing Last Updated: July 23, 2026

DreamRing ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

DreamRing is an entertainment app that simulates "fake" / prank video calls and chats with original AI characters using pre-recorded clips. There are no real people and no real phone calls. You do not need to create an account to use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, email, or contacts. The App collects the following data automatically or through the third-party services listed in Section 3:

  • Device and advertising identifiers — e.g. the Android Advertising ID and similar device identifiers, used for advertising, analytics, and attribution.
  • Approximate location — coarse, IP-derived location inferred by our advertising and analytics partners (not GPS). The App does not access precise/GPS location.
  • App activity / interactions — how you use the App (screens opened, features used, taps) for analytics and ad measurement.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — technical data (device model, OS version, stack traces, performance metrics) to keep the App stable.
  • Purchase status — whether you bought a subscription or other in-app product, processed through Google Play Billing (we do not receive or store your card/payment details).

Photos you choose stay on your device. If you add a photo to create a custom character, that image is used only on your device to build the character and is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties. The camera/photo permission exists solely for this on-device feature.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide and operate the App and remember your settings and preferences on your device;
  • Show and measure advertising, including limiting how often you see an ad;
  • Understand usage and improve features and performance (analytics);
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues;
  • Process and validate in-app purchases and subscriptions;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We share data with the third-party services listed in Section 3 so they can provide advertising, analytics, attribution, and payment processing on our behalf or for their own purposes as described in their policies. We may also disclose information to comply with the law or a valid legal request, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

U.S. state privacy note. Because we allow advertising partners to use identifiers for personalized/targeted advertising, some U.S. state laws (e.g. California's CCPA/CPRA) may treat this as a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. You may opt out of personalized ads using the advertising-ID controls in Section 3.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

  • Consent — for personalized advertising and, where required, analytics.
  • Legitimate interests — core functionality, security, fraud prevention, and product improvement.
  • Contract / legal obligation — processing in-app purchases and meeting legal requirements.

6. Data Retention

We and our partners keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Advertising and analytics identifiers are retained per each partner's retention schedule; crash and diagnostic data are kept for a limited period. Data stored only on your device remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • GDPR (EEA/UK): access, correct, delete, port, object to or restrict processing, and withdraw consent; and to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California) and similar U.S. state laws: know, delete, and correct personal information; opt out of the "sale"/"sharing" of personal information and of targeted advertising; and non-discrimination for exercising your rights.
  • Other regions (e.g. LGPD in Brazil, PIPEDA in Canada): comparable access, correction, and deletion rights.

To exercise a right, contact us at the email in Section 12. Because the App has no accounts, please include your Advertising ID (and device model/OS) in your request so we can identify the relevant data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. Every character is clearly labeled as AI and is not a real person. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the higher minimum age required in your region). If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. Where required, we and our partners rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for these transfers.

10. Data Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
