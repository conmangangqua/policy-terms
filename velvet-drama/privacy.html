Privacy Policy

App: Velvet Drama Effective: 28/05/2026 Last Updated: 28/05/2026

Velvet Drama — Vertical short drama streaming with bite-sized romance, mafia, billionaire and werewolf stories. ("Velvet Drama", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Velvet Drama mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Velvet Drama does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily contact us.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash logs and performance data
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers (e.g., GAID/IDFA where permitted)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address, not GPS) for ad targeting and analytics

1.3 User Content

Velvet Drama does not collect, store, or transmit any user-generated content or media files to our own servers. All such content is stored locally on your device and remains under your control.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze usage trends
  • Enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertising
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as advertising networks and analytics. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network — Privacy Policy
  • AppsFlyer (attribution) — Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

4. Data Retention

We retain non-personal usage and crash data for up to 24 months for analytics purposes, after which it is automatically aggregated or deleted.

5. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services may process data in countries outside your residence, including the United States. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

6. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect information collected through the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Velvet Drama is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA, where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians may contact us using the details in Section 11.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR (EU/UK Users)

If you reside in the EEA, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under GDPR including: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

9. Your Rights Under CCPA / CPRA (California Users)

California residents have rights including: right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Velvet Drama does not sell personal information for monetary value. Contact us to exercise your rights.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in-app or on our website. Your continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
