Privacy Policy

App: LuminAI Effective: July 10, 2026 Last Updated: July 10, 2026

LuminAI ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

LuminAI is a AI photo app. AI Photo Editor to enhance, restyle & transform your shots with LuminAI. You do not need to create an account to use it.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information processed only on your device (not collected by us)

The App's core features run on your device. The following is processed locally and is not collected, transmitted, or stored on our own servers:

  • Camera. With your permission, the App uses the camera when you capture photos or video inside the App; captured media stays on your device.
  • Photos & media. With your permission, the App reads files and media you select; processed on your device.
  • Audio files. With your permission, the App reads audio files you select, on your device.
  • Photos. With your permission, the App reads images you select from your gallery, on your device.
  • Videos. With your permission, the App reads videos you select or download, on your device.
  • Storage. With your permission, the App saves files (such as downloads or exports) to your device storage.
  • Settings and preferences. Your in-app settings are stored only on your device.

You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

1.2 Information collected automatically by third-party services

When you use the App, the third-party services listed in Section 3 may automatically collect:

  • Advertising & device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID / IDFA) and basic device information (model, OS version, app version).
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address (city/region level) — not GPS-based tracking.
  • App activity & diagnostics such as feature usage, session data, and crash reports.

1.3 Information you provide

The App does not require account registration and does not ask for personal information such as your name, email, or phone number, unless you voluntarily contact support.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the App — core features run on-device; remote configuration keeps the App's settings up to date.
  • Advertising and measurement — to serve ads (which fund the free App) and measure ad performance and install attribution.
  • Analytics and improvement — to understand how the App is used and improve features and stability.
  • Fraud prevention, security and compliance — to detect invalid ad traffic and abuse.
  • Legal compliance — to comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. Information is shared only:

Under some U.S. state privacy laws (for example the CPRA), the transfer of identifiers to advertising partners for personalized ads may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" — Section 7 explains how to opt out.
  • For legal reasons, if required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • In a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, in which case this Policy will continue to apply to previously collected data.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Consent — for personalized advertising and, where required, analytics. You may withdraw consent at any time via the in-app consent dialog or system settings.
  • Legitimate interests — for core App operation, security, fraud prevention, non-personalized advertising, and improvement, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where processing is required by law.

6. Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers and keep no user database. Our third-party partners retain the data they collect for the periods stated in their own policies (for example, Google Analytics for Firebase retains user-level data for up to 14 months; crash logs are retained for around 90 days). Data processed on your device (Section 1.1) remains until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, to object to or restrict processing, to withdraw consent, and to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (GDPR/UK GDPR); or to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the "sale"/"sharing" of personal information and targeted advertising without discrimination (CCPA/CPRA and similar U.S. state laws); or equivalent rights under other laws (LGPD, PIPEDA, and others).

To exercise any right, email support@tranquilmind.co. Because the App has no accounts, please include your Android Advertising ID (Settings → Google → Ads) and device model so we and our partners can locate data associated with your device. You can also act directly on your device: reset your Advertising ID, opt out of ads personalization, use the in-app consent dialog, or clear the App's data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 or older and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, contact us at support@tranquilmind.co and we will work with our partners to delete it.
