Child Safety Standards

App: Obsidian Browser Effective: 30/06/2026 Last Updated: 30/06/2026

Obsidian Browser ("Obsidian Browser", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe online environment. This document sets out our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in accordance with the Google Play Child Safety Standards policy and applicable laws.

Zero tolerance. Obsidian Browser strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not tolerate such content or conduct in any part of our service.

1. Our Commitment

We are dedicated to the prevention, detection, and removal of child sexual abuse material and any conduct that exploits or endangers children. We design our products and policies to keep minors safe and to comply fully with all applicable child-protection laws and platform requirements.

2. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Obsidian Browser maintains a strict zero-tolerance approach toward CSAE. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Creating, uploading, sharing, storing, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
  • Grooming, soliciting, or attempting to engage minors for sexual purposes
  • Trafficking, sextortion, or any exploitation of children
  • Linking to or facilitating access to CSAE material through the App

3. Child Safety Standards

To uphold our commitment, we adhere to the following standards:

  • Prohibition in our policies. CSAE is explicitly banned in our Terms of Service and user policies.
  • Reporting mechanism. We provide an accessible in-app and email channel for users to report suspected CSAE content or behavior.
  • Prompt action. We act on credible reports without undue delay, including removing content and restricting offending accounts where applicable.
  • Reporting to authorities. We report confirmed CSAM to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and/or local law enforcement, as required by law.
  • Designated child safety contact. We maintain a point of contact for child-safety matters (see Section 8).

Obsidian Browser is a web browser and does not host user-generated content on our own servers. Where the App is used to access third-party content, we cooperate with platform and legal reporting mechanisms to address CSAE.

4. Reporting CSAE

If you encounter any content or behavior involving the sexual abuse or exploitation of children while using Obsidian Browser, please report it immediately:

  • Email us at contact@tranquilmind.co with the subject line "Child Safety Report".
  • Include as much detail as possible (URLs, descriptions, timestamps) without downloading, storing, or re-sharing the material.

If a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services or law enforcement right away.

5. How We Respond

Upon receiving a report of suspected CSAE, we will:

  • Review the report promptly and prioritize child-safety cases
  • Remove or disable access to violating content where it is within our control
  • Preserve relevant evidence as required by law
  • Report confirmed CSAM to NCMEC and/or appropriate law enforcement agencies
  • Take appropriate action against any account or user responsible for the violation

6. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable child-protection laws and platform requirements, including the Google Play Child Safety Standards policy. We cooperate with law enforcement and recognized child-protection organizations in investigations relating to CSAE.

7. Resources & Hotlines

If you or someone you know needs help, the following organizations provide support and reporting channels:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) — CyberTipline
  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF) — iwf.org.uk
  • INHOPE (international network of hotlines) — inhope.org

8. Contact

For any child-safety concern or to reach our designated child-safety contact, please email:

Email: contact@tranquilmind.co
