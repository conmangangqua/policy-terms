Privacy Policy

App: Mochi Chat Effective: July 23, 2026 Last Updated: July 23, 2026

Mochi Chat ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by Tranquil Mind Technology JSC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices and rights you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Mochi Chat is an AI companion app where you chat by text or voice with AI characters for entertainment and companionship. You do not need to create an account or sign in to use it.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide that stays on your device

During onboarding you may enter a name, age, gender, interests, an email, and choose a profile photo. This profile information is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

Chat content

When you send a message, your message text (and recent conversation context) is transmitted to our AI provider to generate a reply. If you use voice input, your speech is converted to text on your device and only the resulting text is sent. If you use voice replies (text-to-speech), the message text may be sent to a translation service for language detection.

Information collected automatically

When you use the App, we and our partners may collect:

  • Device and advertising identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID, device identifiers).
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address (not GPS) for advertising and analytics.
  • App activity — in-app interactions, features used, and ad interactions.
  • Purchase activity — subscription/purchase events and status (not your full payment card details, which are handled by Google Play).
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — technical information to keep the App stable.

Microphone

With your permission, the microphone is used for the speech-to-text feature. Audio is processed by your device's speech recognition; we do not store or transmit raw audio recordings.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the App and its chat features.
  • Remember your preferences and personalize your experience (on-device).
  • Show and measure advertising, including rewarded ads that unlock features.
  • Analytics, troubleshooting, and improving the App.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and secure the App.
  • Process subscriptions and purchases.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. We share data with the third-party services listed in Section 3 to operate the App, serve and measure advertising, and process purchases; for legal reasons; and in connection with a business transfer. Because we use personalized advertising, some U.S. state privacy laws may treat this as "sharing" or "sale" for targeted-advertising purposes — you can opt out as described in Sections 3 and 7.

5. Legal Bases (EEA/UK — GDPR)

  • Consent — personalized advertising and analytics where consent is required.
  • Legitimate interests — core functionality, security, and improving the App.
  • Contract / legal obligation — processing purchases and meeting legal duties.

6. Data Retention

We and our partners keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. Advertising/analytics identifiers are retained per each partner's retention periods; crash and diagnostic data is kept for a limited period. Profile data stored on your device remains until you delete it or uninstall the App.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, object to, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent.

  • EEA/UK (GDPR): the rights above, plus the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the "sale"/"sharing" of personal information and targeted advertising, and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.
  • Other regions (e.g. LGPD, PIPEDA): equivalent rights may apply.

To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below. Because the App has no account, please include your advertising ID and device details so we can locate the relevant data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 18 and over and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where our service providers operate. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

10. Data Security
